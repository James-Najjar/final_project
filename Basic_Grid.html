Latest Space News

New Exoplanet Discovered

Scientists have discovered a potentially habitable exoplanet orbiting a nearby star. The planet, named Kepler-452b, is being called Earth's "older cousin" due to its similarities to our home world.

Mars Rover Finds Evidence of Ancient Lake

NASA's Perseverance rover has uncovered compelling evidence of an ancient lake on Mars, suggesting that the Red Planet may have once harbored conditions suitable for life.
